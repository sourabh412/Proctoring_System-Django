Tutorial

In case you face any problem while accessing the Virtual Programming Lab platform please go through the stepwise guided tutorial below. The guided instructions should be able to solve your issue. If the problem persists, despite following the guide please contact the respective faculty to guide you with the same or you can report the problem.

Landing Page

This is the first page you will see when you enter the website. Signup with your bmsce mail id to continue.

Signup Page

On clicking the SignUp button, you will be redirected to the following page. Create an account with your BMSCE mail id by entering the secret key given by your proctor.

Student details

Once successfully signed up, you will be redirected to fill in your details. Fill in all the details appropriately.(If any changes, you can request your proctor directly or the admin from the report tab Request admin)

The signup process is completed and you will be redirected to your dashboard.

Login Page

Enter your BMSCE mail id and password to login to your account.

Forgot password

You can reset your password by clicking on the forgot password option.

Reset password

Once you enter your registered mail id, you will recieve an OTP through your BMSCE mail id. Use the OTP to reset your password.

Dashboard

This is the first page you will see after you've successfully logged in. Your details are visiblle on this page.

Uploading profile picture

Profile picture can be update once by clicking on the profile picture icon. The image should be of type Portrait photo with red background(as per dept. instructions) and less than 30Kb in size.

Activites page

New activities can be updated on the activities page. Activites are verified and graded by the respective proctor.

Records page

Records can be updated only once. Students are required to update the courses selected by them.

Updating records

After updating the courses, you will be allowed to update the records for all the courses(only once). The records are then visible as a table for each Semester.
